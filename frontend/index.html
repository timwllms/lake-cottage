Welcome to Your Retreat

Nestled on the shores of a pristine lake, Willow Lake Cottage offers the perfect escape for families and couples seeking peace and natural beauty. Wake up to stunning water views, spend your days kayaking or fishing, and end each evening around the fire pit watching the sun set over the mountains.

Our cozy cottage features modern amenities while maintaining its rustic charm. Whether you're looking for adventure or simply want to unwind with a good book, you'll find everything you need for an unforgettable getaway.

Amenities

  • Private dock & lakefront access
  • Kayaks, canoe & life vests
  • Fully equipped kitchen
  • Washer & dryer
  • WiFi & Smart TV
  • Fire pit & outdoor seating
  • Gas grill & picnic table
  • Linens & towels provided
  • Parking for 2 vehicles
  • Board games & outdoor games
